Payment transparency

Exactly how payment works.

Pay in full

The full evaluation fee is paid before scheduling begins. This is the cleanest option when you want one receipt and no follow-up balance.

Pay half now, half later

The first 50% deposit starts scheduling and evaluator preparation. The final 50% is due at completion before the final report is released.

How card or ACH payment happens

Payment is handled on a secure Helcim-hosted checkout page. HexaHire does not store card numbers, bank details, or payment credentials on this website.

What the listed price includes

The listed price covers the evaluation tier shown, evaluator review, and the written report. Any custom scope, rush delivery, or extra candidates should be confirmed by invoice before payment.

Why Helcim

Chosen to keep your processing cost low.

ACH is cheapest for big invoices

Helcim lists ACH at 0.5% plus 25 cents, capped at $6 for approved transactions below $25,000.

No monthly account fee

Helcim lists no monthly account fees, no setup fees, no minimums, and no cancellation fees.

Card payments stay available

Clients can still pay by credit or debit card when they prefer speed, rewards, or card-based procurement.

Hosted payment pages

Hosted checkout avoids storing card or bank details on this website, which keeps the static site simpler and safer.
